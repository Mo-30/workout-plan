⚡ THE 7 NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES
◆Protein first, always. Every meal must have a protein source. No meal is just bread and vegetables.
◆No liquid calories. Water, black coffee, black/green tea only. Juice and soda are banned.
◆No whey. Hit 160g from eggs, chicken, beef, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, peanut butter.
◆Cravings are redirected, not suppressed. Deploy Sweet Fix. Never go cold turkey.
◆3 meals minimum. Skipping creates binge cycles. Space meals out.
◆Salt is fine. Refined sugar is banned. Salt on real food = healthy. Added sugar in drinks = fat.
◆Daily supplements: 5g Creatine + Omega-3 + Wellman — with first fat-containing meal.
🔑 THE FLEXIBLE MEAL SYSTEM
◆Slot 1 — First Meal. Within 1 hour of waking.
◆Slot 2 — Pre-Workout. 30–90 min before training. Fast carbs + light protein.
◆Slot 3 — Post-Workout Main Meal. Largest meal. Within 60 min of finishing training.
◆Slot 4 — Last Meal. 2–3 hours before sleep. High protein + lower carb.
◆Sweet Fix. After Slot 3 or 4. Built into 1900 kcal. Never skipped, never guilty.

🍫 THE SWEET TOOTH PROTOCOL
Every option fits into 1900 kcal. Pick one per day. Cravings are redirected, never suppressed.
🍦 Diet Ice Cream
70–85 kcal · ~3g protein
Your zero added sugar ice cream. 70–85 kcal per piece depending on flavor. This is the most direct craving kill — it IS ice cream. No substitution needed.
✦ Best deployed after Slot 4 when the dessert craving hits hardest. Keep 2–3 in the freezer at all times. It fits the budget. No guilt, no tracking anxiety.
🥜 PB Banana Toast
~260 kcal · 12g protein
2 slices toast + 1.5 tbsp natural peanut butter + sliced banana + light drizzle of honey. Hits the pastry/chocolate craving and delivers 12g protein.
✦ The PB makes it taste indulgent. It is not. Best fix when you need something that feels like a real treat, not a "diet food."
🥞 Oat Pancakes
~250 kcal · 14g protein
40g oats + 2 eggs + 1 mashed banana — pan-fry, top with 1 tsp honey. Add cinnamon for richer flavor. Tastes like a real dessert. Is a protein meal.
✦ Works as breakfast OR a late-night sweet fix. Make extra and refrigerate — reheats in 90 seconds.
🍯 Greek Yogurt Bowl
~190 kcal · 18g protein
200g Greek yogurt + 1 tsp honey + sliced banana or berries. Destroys ice cream cravings. Freeze for 30 min for a frozen yogurt effect.
✦ Add 1 tbsp peanut butter to this for a chocolate-PB ice cream experience at ~280 kcal, 24g protein.
🍞 Protein French Toast
~270 kcal · 18g protein
2 slices toast dipped in 2 eggs + cinnamon. Pan-fry until golden, top with 1 tsp honey. Tastes like a café pastry. Hits 18g protein.
✦ Best when craving pastries or baked goods. Cinnamon + honey eliminates the craving completely.
🍫 Dark Chocolate (Emergency Only)
~170 kcal · 2g protein
2–3 squares of 70%+ dark chocolate. Not a daily option — a harm-reduction protocol. Exists to prevent a full chocolate binge. Track it in the 1900 kcal budget.
✦ Dark chocolate only. Not milk chocolate, not bars with added sugar.
