⚡ THE 7 NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES
◆Protein first, always. Every meal must contain a protein source. No meal is just bread and vegetables.
◆No liquid calories. Water, black coffee, and black/green tea only. Juice, soda, and sugary drinks are banned — they are your #1 fat-storing enemy.
◆No whey protein. Hit 160g purely from eggs, chicken, beef, Greek yogurt, and cottage cheese.
◆Cravings are solved, not suppressed. When you want sugar, deploy the Sweet Fix protocol (see tab). You never go cold turkey. You redirect.
◆3 meals minimum on regular days. Missing a meal is not discipline — it makes you binge later. Space them out.
◆Salt is your friend. Use it freely on real food. It keeps sodium levels up for pumps and cellular hydration. Refined sugar is the enemy, not salt.
◆Supplements are non-negotiable: 5g Creatine + Omega-3 + Wellman Multivitamin — taken with your first fat-containing meal every day.
📊 MACRO BREAKDOWN
◆Protein — 160g = 640 kcal (34% of diet). From: eggs, chicken, beef, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, tuna (max 1×/week).
◆Carbs — ~160g = 640 kcal (34% of diet). From: rice, oats, bread, fruits, dates. Time them around workouts.
◆Fat — ~68g = 612 kcal (32% of diet). From: eggs, beef, olive oil, Greek yogurt. This keeps testosterone and joints healthy.
🔑 THE FLEXIBLE MEAL SYSTEM
◆Because your training time and schedule varies daily, this plan uses Meal Slots — not clock times. You eat in sequence, not at fixed hours.
◆Slot 1 (First Meal) — within 1 hour of waking. Medium protein + carbs.
◆Slot 2 (Pre-Workout) — only if 2+ hours have passed since Slot 1. Light carbs + protein.
◆Slot 3 (Post-Workout) — largest meal of the day. High protein + high carb. Eat within 60 min of finishing training.
◆Slot 4 (Last Meal) — 2–3 hours before sleep. High protein + lower carb. Keeps muscle-building overnight.
◆Sweet Fix — anytime after Slot 3 or 4. Built into the 1900 kcal. Never skipped, never guilty.

🍫 THE SWEET TOOTH PROTOCOL
Your cravings are real. Suppressing them creates binge cycles. Instead, redirect them. Every option below is built into your 1900 kcal and hits 12–18g protein. Pick one per day.
🥞 Oat Pancakes
~250 kcal · 14g protein
Mix 40g oats + 2 eggs + 1 mashed banana into a batter. Pan-fry in a non-stick pan (no oil). Top with 1 tsp honey. Tastes like a real dessert. Is actually a protein meal.
✦ Add cinnamon to the batter for a richer flavour. Works as breakfast OR as a late-night sweet fix.
🍯 Greek Yogurt Bowl
~190 kcal · 18g protein
200g full-fat Greek yogurt + 1 tsp honey + sliced banana or a handful of berries (fresh or frozen). Thick, cold, and sweet. Destroys ice cream cravings.
✦ Freeze it for 30 min for a "frozen yogurt" effect. Tastes like a cheat meal. Is not one.
🍞 Protein French Toast
~270 kcal · 18g protein
Dip 2 slices toast in a mix of 2 eggs + cinnamon + splash of milk. Pan-fry until golden. Top with 1 tsp honey. Tastes like a pastry from a café. Hits 18g protein.
✦ Best option when you're craving pastries or baked goods. The cinnamon + honey combination neutralizes the craving completely.
🍌 Banana & Dates Shake (No Blender)
~230 kcal · 15g protein
Mash 1 banana + 2 Medjool dates into 150g Greek yogurt. Mix with a fork. Thick, sweet, and filling. The dates are your Sunnah bonus — they count toward your daily 3 limit.
✦ Best post-Iftar sweet fix on fasting days. Natural sugars + slow protein = no spike and crash.
🍫 Dark Chocolate (Emergency Protocol)
~170 kcal · 2g protein
If none of the above options are available and the craving is intense: 2–3 squares of dark chocolate (70%+ cocoa). This is a harm-reduction protocol, not a daily option.
✦ Dark chocolate only — not milk chocolate. Track it in your 1900 kcal budget. This exists so you never go for a full Kit Kat binge.
